Powerful Features

Our AI-powered analyzing tool is designed to give you the most accurate and actionable insights from your resume.

Skill Extraction

Automatically identifies technical and soft skills from your résumé text. We cross-reference over thousands of industry-standard skills to accurately represent your abilities.

Role Matching

Matches your profile against distinct job categories. Using TF-IDF Vectorization and Support Vector Machines (SVM), we accurately classify which role best suits your background.

Phone Extractor

Our intelligent Regex parser efficiently scans through your document to securely extract primary contact numbers for automated hiring workflows.

Email Extraction

Easily isolates email addresses from your uploaded resume formatting so recruiters can quickly gather your contact details in their systems.

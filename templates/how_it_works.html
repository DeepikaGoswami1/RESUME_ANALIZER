How It Works

Follow our simple logic pipeline to see how AI perfectly decodes your uploaded document.

1

Upload Your Resume

Simply upload your resume in PDF or TXT format on our "Analyze Now" page. We ensure your data is processed securely and temporarily.

2

AI Processing Pipeline

Our backend parses the text from your resume, then vigorously cleans out noise (URLs, special characters, extra spacing) using powerful Regular Expressions.

3

Machine Learning Classification

Your cleaned resume is transformed via a custom TF-IDF Vectorizer. A trained Support Vector Machine (SVM) model then predicts the category and job role that perfectly fits your profile.

4

Insights Delivery

Finally, your contact information, exact roles, and hard/soft skills are extracted and presented back to you on our sleek, vibrant 3D dashboard!
